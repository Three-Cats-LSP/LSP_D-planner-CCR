## What's New

### 🔧 Tools Mode
A new Tools button sits next to Rec | Bühlmann. Tap it to enter a dedicated tools workspace with four calculators:

**MOD Calculator** — Drag two sliders (O₂ % and ppO₂ limit) to instantly see the Maximum Operating Depth in metres and feet. Colour-coded result.

**Best Mix** — Set your planned depth and ppO₂ limit to find the optimal gas mix. Shows the O₂ %, actual ppO₂ at depth, and the gas name (EAN 32, EAN 50, etc.).

**Max Depth** — Set any O₂ % to see the maximum safe depth at both 1.4 bar (working) and 1.6 bar (deco) limits.

**Unit Converter** — Five conversion tools: Pressure (Bar/PSI/MPa), Volume (L/ft³), Depth (m/ft), Temperature (°C/°F), Weight (kg/lbs). All bidirectional with live updates.

---

### ⚠️ Contingency Plans
After running a Bühlmann deco plan, a new Contingency Plans section appears below the GF Curve:

- **Gas Loss** — tap "Lose EAN 50" (or whichever gas) to instantly see what your deco looks like without that gas
- **+3 min / +5 min** — see the impact of an extended bottom time on your deco obligation

Your original plan is always preserved.

---

### 📖 Reference Modal
The Reference tab has been replaced with a `📖 REF` button next to the Training Use Only warning. Tap it from any mode to open a clean overlay with algorithm info, depth limits, and the Instagram QR code.
